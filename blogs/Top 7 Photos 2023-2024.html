My Top 7 Photos of 2023-24

(Technically 2023-08/2024)

Lasted Edited August 14th By Spencer Ye



The following eight photos are my favorite for a wide range of reasons. I will admit there is a sense of recency bias here, and a lot more photos are recent to when I made this in Mid-August, 2024.




Me and my best friends from elementary school at graduation. We've been friends for more than a decade, and the four of us have enjoyed the highs and supported each other through the lows, and this moment really captured the feeling of hitting a milestone together.






Me and one of my best friends at college hugging goodbye during Track Senior Night. Going into college, my biggest fear was not making good friends, since I don't consider myself the most extroverted person. For me, this photo really meant: Yes, I was able to make amazing friends. Paraphrasing Winnie the Pooh, "How lucky are we to have people who make saying goodbye so hard".






LOL I love this one. This is me laughing while posing like a mugshot after getting a parking ticket. As I was driving on an average Saturday, smoke started billowing out of my engine and my car stopped driving on the side of a parking lot. I ultimately got a parking ticket for being outside of the lines. I was initially upset at getting a ticket, but I just laughed it off. I'm generally proud of my ability to deal with these kind of setbacks with humor.






These are the two balls I used to hit my first par and birdies in golf. I picked up golf in Summer 2024 to get the chance to spend more time with my dad. I'm very proud of this, because I was able to put my mind towards improving at something I sucked at, and it happened.






I love cooking; I've been cooking since I was 10. This was my first steak I cooked for my family (along with the side dishes) It was VERY good.






I was lucky enough that my #1 Artist for the past 3 years, Martin Garrix, hosted a couple shows in San Francisco. A lot of his music is connected to a lot of fond memories in my life, and his euphoric style of music never fails to energize me in the morning.






An absolute beautiful east coast sunset in Baltimore. East coast sunsets are different than those from home in the west coast. This moment made me realize I made the right decision to choose a school all the way across the country, because it was simply a different environment than what was usual and comfortable to me.
